Why we built this

The meal you loved deserves more than a faded memory.

We've all been there — a restaurant recommendation half-remembered from a conversation six months ago, a dish so good you promised yourself you'd go back, a neighbourhood you explored once and never found again.

Catalog is the app for people who care. Not just about eating, but about the experience of eating — the context, the company, the feeling of finding somewhere that feels exactly right. We built it because we needed it, and we think you do too.
